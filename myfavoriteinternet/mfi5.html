Design & Authoring for Interactive Media

My Favorite Internet 5

The Scream by Edvard Munch

To make this painting interactive on a website, the waves could ripple when your cursor moves over it. When your cursor moves over the face or mouth, it could change expression.

The Persistence of Memory by Salvador Dali

This piece could be interactive in various ways. When moving the cursor over a clock, it could slide down a bit. You could click and drag the clock hands to change the time. The user could decide where the clocks go by dragging them to different areas of the image.
